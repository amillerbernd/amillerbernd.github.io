About me

Hi there, I'm Annie. I write, report, analyze and visualize data, craft and execute content strategy and design things. All of that is pretty exclusive to the internet and in no particular order.

Previously, I helped the San Antonio Express-News build out its digital strategy, coordinated training for reporters and did some data reporting. Before that, I covered breaking news in California.

I live and eat tacos in San Antonio, Texas with my boyfriend and our Chihuahua, who thinks she's a German Shepherd.
